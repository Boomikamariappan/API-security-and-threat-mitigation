Our Technology

Our proprietary security platform combines multiple layers of protection using advanced machine learning algorithms trained on petabytes of network data and threat intelligence.

Deep Learning Engine

Neural networks analyze traffic patterns in real-time, identifying anomalies with sub-millisecond latency. Our models are trained on over 10 million known attack vectors.

Behavioral Analysis

Continuous monitoring of network behavior establishes baselines and detects deviations that may indicate compromise, including zero-day threats.

Cloud-Based Protection

Distributed nodes across 15 global regions provide DDoS mitigation with automatic traffic rerouting and scrubbing capabilities.

Threat Intelligence

Integrated with 50+ threat feeds and our own honeypot network, updating defenses against emerging threats every 30 seconds.

Automated Response

AI-driven incident response automatically contains threats while providing human-readable forensic reports.

Encrypted Traffic Analysis

Patented technology inspects encrypted traffic without decryption, maintaining privacy while detecting threats.
